About Draftivist

Advocacy isn’t always easy.

It can be hard to know what policymakers to reach out to, when to reach out to them, and what exactly to say when you do. And, how can you make sure your voice is being heard?

Meet Draftivist — a free tool to help progressive activist groups engage with their communities at scale, educate them on localized issues, and empower them to message local officials in minutes.

Draftivist is designed to help citizens’ emails get past politicians’ inbox filters and keep them on-message with larger movements, while representing each individual’s values and voice. After hitting send, Draftivist can also show people how they can get further involved with the movement at hand.

Draftivist is 100% developed by volunteers, and is 100% free to use.

Draftivist will not log any personal user data, and will be radically transparent in its data policy.

If you’re an activist group interested in using Draftivist — or if you’re an individual looking to lend your hand to help build it — please reach out to us at draftivist@gmail.com.
