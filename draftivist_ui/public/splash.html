About Draftivist

Advocacy isn’t always easy.

It can be hard to know what policymakers to reach out to, when to reach out to them, and what exactly to say when you do—and how do you know if your voice is even being heard?

Meet Draftivist: a free tool to help activist groups engage with their communities at scale, educate them on localized issues, and empower them to message their local officials in minutes.

Draftivist is designed to help citizens’ emails get past politicians’ inbox filters and keep them on-message with larger movements—while still representing each individual’s values and voice. After hitting send, citizens can also learn how to get involved and further support the issue at hand.

Draftivist is 100% developed by volunteers, and will be 100% free to use. Draftivist will not log personal user data, and will have a radically transparent data policy.

If you’re an activist group interested in using Draftivist, or if you’re someone who’d like to lend a hand to help build it, please reach out to us at draftivist@gmail.com.
